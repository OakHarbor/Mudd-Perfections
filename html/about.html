About Mudd Perfections

My name is Alex Hernandez, and as the owner of Mudd Perfections LLC I want to make sure that you know what my business is all about. More than twenty years ago, I began my work in the construction industry installing and painting drywall. After eight years of mastering my craft, I decided it was time to start my own contracting business to better service the Dorr, MI area with quality wall construction. Alongside my experienced team of contractors, I provide drywall installation services to a standard of excellence.

Nothing is more important to me than providing my customers with the beautiful drywall that people have come to expect from me. I have spent every workday of the last twelve years expanding my knowledge and improving my practice so that I can give each and every customer the highest quality drywall they can find. All of the members of my contracting team have years of experience with their specialty, so when you call me for any job you can be sure that only the most skilled professionals will be working for you.

Under the Mudd Perfections LLC name I provide service throughout the Dorr area, including to Dorr, Hamilton, Holland, Zeeland, and Burnips. There is no other contracting company in the area that can provide the standard of service that I can, so even if you live outside of city limits I will still drive out to serve you. With my acoustical ceilings, you will see a noticeable difference in the way your home or office carries sound, so give me a call today for one of my free estimates.
